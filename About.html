About all the LE SERRAFIM members

Kim Chaewon (김채원; or simply Chaewon) is a South Korean singer-songwriter under Source Music. She is the leader of the girl group LE SSERAFIM and a former member of the project girl group IZ*ONE. She is likely the most popular member of LE SERRAFIM. Due to her notoriety with fans worldwide even beyond Kpop.
