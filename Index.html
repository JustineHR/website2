What is LE SERRAFIM?

LE SSERAFIM (르세라핌) is a five-member girl group under Source Music. Originally as six, they debuted on May 2, 2022 with their first mini album Fearless.
The group's name is an anagram of "I'm Fearless", and it implies self-confidence and strong will to move forward without being swayed by the world's gaze.
